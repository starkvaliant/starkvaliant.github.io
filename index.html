About Me

I've loved the possibilities of the internet ever since the barrage of AOL CDs in the 90's. With great excitement, I collected those CDs "just in case we ran out of free hours", until I found out they didn't work that way - at which point I switched gears and decided I was going to cover a wall of my bedroom with the collection. (Luckily, the 90's ended and I never followed that bad idea through.)

I'm Stephanie Stark, hailing from the sunny state of Florida, specifically the friendly little town of Niceville. I attended Flagler College in St. Augustine, Florida, from 2005 to 2009, and graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design. During my studies, I took a course in Web Design, returning to TA the same class next semester due to my interest in it. This interest combined with my design background led me to seek out opportunities in the web field.

Currently, I'm a senior front-end web developer/web designer at Naked Lime, in College Station, Texas, where I design and build web solutions for automotive customers across the nation. I also assist in product development and testing.
